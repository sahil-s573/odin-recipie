Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is an aromatic, flavorful mixed rice dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It combines fragrant long-grain basmati rice, tender spiced meats (like chicken, mutton, or beef), or vegetables, and is traditionally slow-cooked with saffron, yogurt, and authentic spices.

ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee (or oil)
  • Whole spices
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Herbs

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Boil the Rice
  3. Brown the Onions
  4. Cook the Chicken Base
  5. Layer
  6. "Dum" (Steam) Cook
  7. Serve
