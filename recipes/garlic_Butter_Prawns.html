Garlic Butter Prawns

Description

Garlic Butter Prawns is a fast, savory dish featuring tender prawns seared in a rich, velvety butter sauce. The sauce blends heavy garlic and bright lemon juice to create a tangy, deeply savory glaze. This versatile meal pairs perfectly with crusty bread, steamed rice, or pasta.

ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Melt thearnish and serve: butter
  2. Sauté the garlic
  3. Cook the prawns
  4. Flip and finish cooking
  5. Garnish and serve
